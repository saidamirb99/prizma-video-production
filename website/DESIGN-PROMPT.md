## Design Direction

### Primary Style: Glassmorphism + Neumorphism Fusion
Create a **dark-themed cinematic website** that combines:

1. **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass cards, translucent panels with backdrop-blur, gradient borders, layered depth with semi-transparent surfaces over video/image backgrounds
2. **Neumorphism**: Soft raised/inset UI elements (buttons, stat counters, form inputs), subtle inner/outer shadows on dark surfaces, tactile feel for interactive elements
3. **Cinematic touches**: Full-screen video hero, parallax scrolling, smooth reveal animations, dramatic lighting effects via CSS gradients

### Color Palette
- **Background**: Deep black (#0a0a0f) to dark navy (#0d1117)
- **Glass panels**: rgba(255,255,255,0.05) to rgba(255,255,255,0.1) with blur
- **Accent gradient**: Electric violet (#7c3aed) → Cyan (#06b6d4) → Magenta (#ec4899)
- **Text**: White (#ffffff) primary, rgba(255,255,255,0.7) secondary
- **Glow effects**: Accent colors with blur for hover states and highlights

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, uppercase, wide tracking — modern geometric sans-serif (e.g., Syne, Unbounded, or Space Grotesk)
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar)
- **Accent numbers**: Extra bold, large scale for statistics

### Animations & Interactions
- Smooth scroll with sections revealing via fade-up / slide-in
- Glass cards with subtle hover lift + glow border
- Neumorphic buttons that press inward on click
- Video thumbnails with play overlay and scale-on-hover
- Counter animation for statistics section
- Parallax depth on hero background
- Navigation blur effect on scroll
